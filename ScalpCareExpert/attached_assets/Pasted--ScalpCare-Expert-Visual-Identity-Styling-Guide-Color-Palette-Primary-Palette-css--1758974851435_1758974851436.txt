## Design Principles

**1. Scandinavian Minimalism**
- White space is our most important design element
- Functionality before decoration
- Clean lines and geometric shapes
- Subtle shadows and minimal depth

**2. Natural Trustworthiness**
- Organic forms inspired by leaves and growth
- Textures reminiscent of natural materials
- Authentic photography over stock images
- Earthy colors that feel calming

**3. Medical Precision**
- Grid-based layout for structure
- Consistent spacing (8px base unit system)
- Clear hierarchy and navigation
- Data-driven content presentation

## Image Guidelines

**Photo Style:**
- **Natural lighting** always preferred
- **Authentic people** - no over-styled models
- **Minimal retouching** - preserves natural skin texture
- **Neutral backgrounds** - focus on the person
- **Before/after images** must be same angle and lighting

**Product Imagery:**
- **Clean white backgrounds** for product images
- **45-degree angles** for dimensional depth
- **Consistent shadows** for cohesion
- **Macro details** of textures and ingredients

## Iconography

**Style:**
- **Line-based icons** (2px stroke weight)
- **Rounded endpoints** for softness
- **24x24px grid** for consistency
- **Minimal color** - primarily black or primary green

**ScalpCare Expert Icons:**
- Leaf motifs for natural ingredients
- Microscope for scientific approach
- Clock for treatment duration
- Checkmarks for results
- Profile silhouettes for before/after

## Brand Voice in Design

**ScalpCare Expert Design Tone:**
- **Professional yet accessible** - clean lines, readable fonts
- **Natural and authentic** - organic shapes, real photography
- **Safe and reliable** - consistent spacing, familiar patterns
- **Premium but not arrogant** - subtle luxury, not ostentatious

## Logo Guidelines

**ScalpCare Expert Logo Principles:**
- **Wordmark-based** with clean typography
- **Subtle leaf/growth element** integrated into letters
- **Primary version** in dark green on light backgrounds
- **Secondary version** in white on dark backgrounds
- **Minimum size:** 120px width for digital, 25mm for print

**Logo Variations:**
```css
/* Horizontal Logo */
.logo-horizontal: used in headers and footers

/* Stacked Logo */
.logo-stacked: used when width is constrained

/* Icon Version */
.logo-icon: only for social media profile pictures and favicons
```
